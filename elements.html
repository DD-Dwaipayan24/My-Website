Definition

Supervised Learning

A type of machine learning where models are trained on labeled data. Common algorithms include linear regression, SVMs, and neural networks.

Unsupervised Learning

Involves learning from data without explicit labels. Techniques such as clustering and dimensionality reduction fall under this category.

Reinforcement Learning

Focuses on agents taking actions in environments to maximize cumulative reward through trial and error.
